## Award-Label Caution

The exemplar set intentionally mixes user-recognized papers, official venue pages, and recent best/outstanding/award-style papers. Before claiming an award in a paper, slide, or public text, verify the current venue award page. The skill uses these papers for writing technique, not for bibliographic status claims.

## Practical Interpretation

The public reviewer guidelines emphasize recurring review concerns: originality or novelty, quality or soundness, clarity or presentation, significance or contribution, evidence strength, reproducibility, and responsible handling of limitations. This skill encodes those concerns as writing and self-review modules rather than as generic advice.

## Update Rule

Before a real submission, verify the target venue's current author instructions, review criteria, page limits, anonymity policy, supplementary material rules, artifact policy, and ethics requirements. Conference rules change year to year.
