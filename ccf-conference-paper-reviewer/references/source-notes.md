# Source Notes

Use these notes when explaining the rationale for the review method or when checking current official venue rules. Verify official pages again when the user asks for latest-year requirements.

## Official Venue Criteria

- AAAI-26 main technical track says submissions are evaluated for significance and novelty of contributions, theoretical and/or empirical soundness, relevance to the AAAI community, and clarity. It also calls out responsible research and reproducibility. Source: https://aaai.org/conference/aaai/aaai-26/main-technical-track-call/
- AAAI-26 submission instructions state that reproducibility checklist answers are shared with reviewers and that reproducibility assessment is weighed in final decisions. Source: https://aaai.org/conference/aaai/aaai-26/submission-instructions/
- NeurIPS 2026 reviewer guidelines use the core dimensions of quality, clarity, significance, and originality, interpreted by contribution type. Source: https://neurips.cc/Conferences/2026/ReviewerGuidelines
- ICLR 2026 reviewer guide frames review as judging whether a submission brings sufficient value to the community and contributes new knowledge. Source: https://iclr.cc/Conferences/2026/ReviewerGuide
- ACL Rolling Review review form includes soundness, excitement/usefulness, reproducibility, ethics, reviewer confidence, strengths, concerns, and questions. Source: https://aclrollingreview.org/reviewform
- CVPR reviewer guidance and training materials emphasize technical quality, responsible reviewing, novelty/quality distinctions, reviewer discussion, and standard review errors. Sources: https://cvpr.thecvf.com/Conferences/2026/ReviewerGuidelines and https://cvpr.thecvf.com/Conferences/2026/ReviewerTrainingMaterial

## CS Paper Review Methods

- S. Keshav's "How to Read a Paper" recommends a three-pass approach and asks the five Cs after the first pass: category, context, correctness, contributions, and clarity. Source: https://www.cs.jhu.edu/~huang/cs624/spring22/readings/paper-reading.pdf
- CMU's "How to Review a Technical Paper" emphasizes technical approach and analysis, computation, related research, clarity, and constructive review structure. Source: https://www.cs.cmu.edu/afs/cs/academic/class/15828-s98/www/review.html

## CSPaper-Inspired Practices

- CSPaper Review describes rubric-faithful, conference-specific AI review for CS papers with actionable feedback. Source: https://cspaper.org/openprint/20260212.0001v1
- CSPaper's Review Rank describes calibrated scores and relative standing within a same-agent, same-venue/track review pool. Use this as inspiration for approximate relative interpretation, but do not claim exact percentiles without a real calibration dataset. Source: https://cspaper.org/articles/introducing-review-rank

## Method Implications

- Use official venue criteria for dimensions and wording.
- Use CS reading/review methods for coverage and flaw detection.
- Use CSPaper-style calibration only as a conceptual model: combine textual review, sub-scores, overall stance, and venue context into a consistent decision signal.
- Convert every deduction into a revision action so the review can feed a writing skill.
